Timmins, Ontario - A new wave of exploration is underway in the Timmins area, as aging gold deposits spark renewed interest among mining companies. With some of the world's largest gold mines located here, some of which have been in operation for over a century, the area is experiencing a new boom as companies seek to find new reserves in the region's "golden zone." While some of the world's best-known gold mines are located in the area, geologists believe there may be even larger deposits yet to be discovered. The new wave of exploration is expected to create jobs and boost the local economy.
