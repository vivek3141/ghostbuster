IBM has announced that it is expecting solid Q3 earnings, despite facing delays in shipments due to supply chain disruptions caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. The company also stated that currency impacts on its earnings will be minimal. IBM has been focusing on shifting towards cloud computing and artificial intelligence, and this strategy has helped the company maintain its financial performance. The ongoing pandemic has accelerated the needs of digital transformation, and IBM's solutions have been well-poised to address those needs. This successful Q3 performance is a testament to IBM's resilience and strategic vision for the future.
