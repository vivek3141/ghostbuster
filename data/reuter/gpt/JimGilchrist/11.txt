Evergreen International Airlines has announced a significant increase in the number of flights from Hong Kong due to high demand. The airline has reported an upsurge in bookings from passengers looking to travel to the United States and Europe. As a result, they plan to add two more flights per week, bringing the total number of flights to six.
The airline is known for its affordable fares and high-quality services, which make it a popular choice for budget travelers who want to explore different parts of the world without breaking the bank. The additional flights will not only make the trip more accessible to passengers but will also help boost the economic activity in the region.
Evergreen International Airlines stated that they take the safety and security of their passengers seriously and have instituted rigorous health and safety protocols to ensure the safety of passengers and crew members. The additional flights are set to commence next month and are expected to be fully booked up quickly.
