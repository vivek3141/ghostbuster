Associated British Foods is expected to announce a significant boost in profits for its retail and textile business segment, according to industry insiders. The conglomerate, which owns popular brands such as Primark and Twinings, has remained resilient amidst a challenging economic climate. Additionally, the textile division's manufacturing and distribution operations have experienced notable growth, further fueling investor optimism. As investors eagerly await the annual report, experts predict the conglomerate's profits to reflect an impressive surge, solidifying its position in the highly competitive retail and textile industries.
