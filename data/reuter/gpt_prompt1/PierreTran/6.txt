By aligning its construction operations with its strategic goals, Compagnie Generale des Eaux aims to leverage its expertise in sustainable development and contribute to France's ambitious environmental targets.
The reorganization plans also involve restructuring the workforce in line with the new business unit's objectives. However, the company emphasized its commitment to minimizing the impact on employees, stating that it will seek to reassign affected workers within the organization whenever possible.
