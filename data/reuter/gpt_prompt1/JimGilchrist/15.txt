Hong Kong's air cargo handling in August witnessed a commendable 6.7% rise compared to the same period last year. The city's bustling air freight industry reported strong numbers, further solidifying its position as a key global logistics hub. Factors such as increased e-commerce activities and a recovering global economy have contributed to this growth. Despite the ongoing pandemic-related challenges, Hong Kong's strategic location and well-developed infrastructure continue to attract businesses and drive trade. The rise in air cargo handling reflects the resilience and adaptability of Hong Kong's logistics sector, playing a vital role in facilitating global supply chains efficiently.
