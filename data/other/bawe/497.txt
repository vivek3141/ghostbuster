Economic stability is the absence of the inefficient aggregate fluctuation in the inflation rate, economic growth, level of employment rate and the balance of payment. If we believe a market economy is itself unstable, then stabilisation policy is introduced as a set of monetary and fiscal policy, designed to attenuate the fluctuations. In this assay, I am going to make a thorough discussion on how the policy should be used to stabilise the economy with examples from the UK.
