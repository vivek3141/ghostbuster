The COVID 19 pandemic has had an impact on the world affecting public health, the economy and various aspects of society. One area that has been particularly affected is nursing, as discussed in Fauteuxs article titled "COVID 19; Impact on Nurses and Nursing." This article sheds light on the changes in nursing practices. The challenges faced by nurses during these unprecedented times through its abstract, introduction, research method and results.
The abstract of the article provides a summary of the main points covered in the research. It highlights how nurses and nursing have been impacted by the pandemic and their valuable contributions to care during this crisis. The abstract also outlines some of the challenges faced by healthcare professionals including shortages of protective equipment (PPE) and increased workloads leading to burnout.
In its introduction the article sets the stage for discussions by providing a comprehensive overview of the COVID 19 pandemic and its effects on healthcare systems. It delves into challenges faced by nurses such as higher risks of infection ethical dilemmas, in providing care and psychological tolls resulting from witnessing high mortality rates. The introduction also emphasizes how understanding these impacts can help improve nursing practices and support nurses well being.
The article takes an approach to research methodology, where interviews with nurses working on the frontlines during the pandemic were conducted. This method allows for an exploration of nurses experiences and perspectives providing a better understanding of the challenges they face. The interviews shed light on changes in nursing practices, such as implementing infection control measures adapting to new protocols quickly and relying more on telehealth.
The studys results offer insights into the changes and challenges in nursing practices during the COVID 19 pandemic. The article emphasizes how nurses endure mental strain due to long working hours, higher patient acuity and exposure to the virus. It also highlights the importance of support systems and resources to ensure their well being. Additionally it underscores how policy changes and resource allocation are crucial in addressing challenges faced by nurses.
In summary "COVID 19; Impact on Nurses and Nursing" significantly contributes to understanding shifts, in nursing practices and the hardships faced by nurses during this pandemic.
The abstract, introduction, research methods and findings all offer insights, into how COVID 19 has affected the nursing field. This information can assist policymakers and healthcare professionals in addressing the evolving demands and ensuring the well being of nurses during these times.
