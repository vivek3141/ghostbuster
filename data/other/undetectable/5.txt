Hong Kong based airline, Cathay Pacific has accomplished the transportation of a valuable shipment consisting of breeding pigs to China. Recently the airline obtained approval from the Hong Kong government to transport these prized pigs. The purpose behind this endeavor is to enhance pork production in China by utilizing these pigs for breeding purposes.
To ensure the well being and safety of the pigs they were transported in a controlled environment with strict adherence to specific conditions. Cathay Pacific made arrangements within one of its freighter aircraft to accommodate these animals comfortably. Special crates were designed to keep them at ease throughout the journey. The airline worked closely with animal experts who ensured that utmost care was taken during their transportation.
The pigs were loaded onto the plane at Hong Kong International Airport. Flown to Beijing, where they received a warm welcome from Chinese government officials. From there they were transported to their destination in central China where they will contribute towards producing top quality pork.
This successful operation serves as a milestone for Cathay Pacific as it underscores their expertise in handling valuable and sensitive cargo. Moreover it highlights the increasing demand for high quality pork in China and Cathay Pacifics commitment, towards supporting the countrys sector.
Cathay Pacific currently operates a thriving air cargo business catering to markets worldwide.
The airline has a wealth of knowledge and experience in transporting animals making it well prepared to fulfill the increasing need for top notch livestock, in Asia and other regions.
