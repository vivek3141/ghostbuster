Regarding. (preposition),
a word used especially in business letters to introduce the particular subject you are writing about.

Emerge. (v)(intansitive),
to appear after being hidden.

Prominent. (adj),
famous or important.

Cultivate. (v)(transitive),
to work hard to develop a particular skill, quality, or attitude.

Consistent. (adj),
always happening in the same way or having the same attitude, quality, etc.

Trend. (n),
the way a situation is generally developing or changing.

Speculate. (intransitive),
to buy goods, property, etc., hoping to make a larg profit when you sell them.
