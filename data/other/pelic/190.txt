What it means to be successful for stamp collecting
 In my opinion, that you've been getting funs and enjoy this activity when you collect the stamps means you're successful for it. Such as, you study how to distinguish the good stamps and normal stamps, then buy them carefully just like taking an important test. After that you put this stamps into the albums and look at them over and again. You are so interested to it, and feel that you've got a great achievement. So you succeed when you are happy with your hobby, collecting stamps.
