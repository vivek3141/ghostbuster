Regarding (prep) a word used especially in business letters to introduce the particular subject you are writing about.

emerge (v) to appear after being hidden.

prominent (adj) famous or important.

cultivate (v) to work hard to develop a particular skill, quality, or attitude.

consistent (adj) approving always happening in the same way or having same attitudes, quality.

trend (n) the way a situation is generally developing or changing.

speculate (v) to guess why something happened or what will happen next, without knowing all the facts.
