The downtown area of Decasia is rapidly decaying. Many of the stores are moving out. For example, In January, Rose's Giant Boutique and Heartland Department Store moved to the Town Mall shopping center. Several sleazy places which is decaying the downtown have opened up. For instance, an "adult"(pornographic) bookstore moved into the building formerly occupied by Rose's Giant Boutique at March 19. In September, an "adult" movie theater opened at the old Chandler Theater which was elegant and closed down in April.
