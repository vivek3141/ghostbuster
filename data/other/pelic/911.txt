We are very difficult to say that which sport is the most popular one in the United State. More people watch baseball than any another sport because there are so many professional baseball games (each team plays 162 games per year). However, the average attendance figure for professional football games are more popular then baseball although there are only 16 games for each team per year. Basketball is the other one popular sport in there especially in big cities. In addition, there are several other sports have millions of fans for example, ice hockey, soccer, golf and tennis. There is no single sport that dominates the whole country.
