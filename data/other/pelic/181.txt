A successful soccer player is that one who is always doing exercises and trying to improve your skills, like running, physical gym and ball practice. Also, the soccer player needs to have a good coach to watch and tell him what exercises and practices the player needs to do. A victorious team and a professional manager is very important to the progress of the player in the soccer world, because he needs to have a good contract to transfer to another clubs and rise the player reputation. In constrast, a victorius team player is not always a good player, the successful player is that ones that have credit with the coach and with the people that believe in the team that the player is working.
