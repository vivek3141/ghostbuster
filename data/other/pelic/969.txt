Suspend (v) to officially stop someone from working, driving, or going to school for a fixed period, because s/he has broken the rules.

Focus (v) to give all your attention to a particular thing.

Alternative (adj) an alternative plan, idea, etc. can be used instead of another one.

Option (n) a choice you can make in a particular situation.

Admonish (v) to tell someone that s/he has done something wrong.

Monitor (n) a piece of equipment that looks like a television and that shows information or pictures, especially on a computer.

come down to (phrasal verb) if a difficult or confusing situation comes down to something, that is single most important thing.
