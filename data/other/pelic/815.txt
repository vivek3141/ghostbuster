The most students are young people, and when you are a student you always have a lot of things to do, and the last thing that you want to do is study. In contrast, study maybe it is the most important thing that you have to do for your future.
 So, when we talk about study, it is very important to manage your time to do all the homework, reviews, readings and lectures.
 You can start in your home; manage your free time and pick some hours to do all your homework. Here are some good steps to follow:
- Sleep 8 hours a day to stay alert and don't feel sleepy.
- Eat healthy food contribute to a better focus and concentration.
- Study in a calm place, with no sound and no television
- Do the homework in the day that the teachers ask for.
