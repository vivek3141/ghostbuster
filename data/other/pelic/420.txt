A subculture in Saudi Arabia is women who work outside the home. There are many women work outside the home. They are teachers, nurses, doctors and professors In the university and they have families. Their children go to school, and they go to work. After work they go home, and they have time to do their household chores. For example, they get up at 6:00 a.m. and prepare breakfast for their children and husbands and go to work. At 1:30 p.m., they come back from work. So, they have 
a long time to take care of their families.
