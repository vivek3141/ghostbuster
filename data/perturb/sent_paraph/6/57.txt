Once upon a time, young Lena discovered the magical world of drabbles. She was excited to create her own masterpiece. A lost princess, a daring knight, and a dragon were told by her. She was able to see vivid scenes in her mind. Each carefully chosen word held immense power, captivating readers with its brevity. As her journey continued, Lena discovered the art of crafting emotions and suspense within this confined space. The world of imagination was unlocked in just 100 words with each drabble.
