Elara discovered that she could possess dragons. As a child, she soared through the skies on their majestic wings, her heart fluttering with unimaginable joy. Elara realized a darker truth as time went on. The magnificent creatures were being hunted by a greedy king. Determined to protect her friends, Elara mustered her strength. She fought against tyranny with her mind linked to the dragons. With each possession, she gave her all, fighting not only for freedom but for the survival of an ancient bond.
