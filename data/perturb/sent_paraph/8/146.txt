The ability to possess dragons was discovered by a young girl named Elara. She was a child when she flew through the skies. Elara realized a darker truth as time went on. The magnificent creatures were being hunted by a greedy king. Determined to protect her friends, Elara mustered her strength. With her mind linked to the dragons, she waged a battle against tyranny. She gave her all, fighting for the survival of an ancient bond, with each possession.
