The company has responded to this trend by investing heavily in digital products, but its revenues have been hit hard in the transition. BSkyB, on the other hand, is one of the UK's largest media companies, with a strong track record in developing and monetizing digital content. Despite these uncertainties, the rumors of a potential takeover bid by BSkyB have put Pearson's shares firmly in the spotlight.
