In a faraway realm, a young girl named Elara discovered her extraordinary gift: the ability to possess dragons. As a child, she soared through the skies on their majestic wings, her heart fluttering with unimaginable joy. Elara realized a darker truth as time went on. These magnificent creatures were being hunted mercilessly by a greedy king, desiring power and control. Determined to protect her friends, Elara mustered her strength. She fought against tyranny with her mind. She gave her all, fighting for the survival of an ancient bond, with each possession.
