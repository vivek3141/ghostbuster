In a faraway realm, a young girl named Elara discovered her extraordinary gift: the ability to possess dragons. She was a child when she flew through the skies on their majestic wings. But as time passed, Elara realized a darker truth. The magnificent creatures were being hunted by a greedy king. Determined to protect her friends, Elara mustered her strength. With her mind linked to the dragons, she waged a battle against tyranny. She gave her all, fighting for the survival of an ancient bond, with each possession.
