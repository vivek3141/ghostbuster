Once upon a time, young Lena discovered the magical world of drabbles. She wanted to make her own work. With only 100 words at her disposal, she weaved a tale of a lost princess, a daring knight, and a mischievous dragon. Words danced on the page, painting vivid scenes in her mind. Each carefully chosen word was captivating. As her journey continued, she discovered the art of crafting emotions and suspense. With each drabble, Lena's passion for storytelling flourished, unlocking a world of imagination in just 100 words.
