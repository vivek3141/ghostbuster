The world of drabbles was discovered by a young girl. She was excited to create her own masterpiece. A lost princess, a daring knight, and a dragon were told by her. She was able to see things that weren't familiar to her. Each carefully chosen word held immense power, captivating readers with its brevity. As her journey continued, Lena discovered the art of crafting emotions and suspense within this confined space. The world of imagination was unlocked in 100 words.
