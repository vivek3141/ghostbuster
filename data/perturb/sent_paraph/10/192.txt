From ancient ages, humans have used symbols and myths to express their experience of a reality that transcends the physical world and transmits it to future generations. mythology is applied to successful products due to their magical power. The logo of the house of Versace is a very extravagant and extraordinary mark; it was designed in 1978 by Gianni Versace. The head of the ancient Greek mythology is represented by the logo. The designer explained why he chose this character; it is a synthesis of beauty and simplicity, which is able to mesmerize anyone, just like the clothes produced by the brand. Other examples are Lamborghini; first, the Italian company was engaged in the production of tractors. The bull is the sacred animal of Zeus and became the emblem of the brand. According to some people, this animal is extremely hardy and strong, which endows the brand with great power. The emblem of the golden bull is very appropriate for the high-performance, expensive cars of Lamborghini. Good and justice win in the myth of Prometheus. The sacred fire from Olympus was taken by Prometheus. The gods were made independent by him. Prometheus introduced people to metals. Humans became proficient in the art of medicine after the titan taught them how to build ships. Prometheus was destroying people’s faith in the deity, and for this, the thunderer Zeus decided to punish him. Prometheus was chained to a rock, the sunburned him mercilessly, and the rain was pounding him. However, this was not the end of the titan’s torment; a giant eagle sat on Prometheus’s chest every day and tore his liver (Travis, 2021). Heracles, the strongest men, killed the eagle that tortured Prometheus, broke Prometheus’ chains, and the titan was free.
