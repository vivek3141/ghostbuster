A young girl named Elara discovered the ability to possess dragons. She was a child when she flew through the skies on their majestic wings. As time went on, Elara realized a darker truth. The magnificent creatures were being hunted by a greedy king. Determined to protect her friends, Elara mustered her strength. She fought against tyranny with her mind linked to the dragons. She fought for the survival of the ancient bond with each possession.
