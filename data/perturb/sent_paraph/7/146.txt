In a faraway realm, a young girl named Elara discovered her extraordinary gift: the ability to possess dragons. As a child, she soared through the skies on their majestic wings, her heart fluttering with unimaginable joy. But as time passed, Elara realized a darker truth. These magnificent creatures were being hunted mercilessly by a greedy king, desiring power and control. Elara wanted to protect her friends. She fought against tyranny with her mind. She gave her all, fighting for the survival of an ancient bond, with each possession.
