According to  industry analysts, semiconductor stocks are set for a comeback as double-digit growth returns. After a slump earlierthis year, semiconductor companies have shown strong gains in Q3, with stocks like NVIDIA and Intel seeing huge jumps. This growth is expected to continue into the end of the year, with high demand for electronics driving sales. Analysts also point to the increased use of chips in automobiles and the growth of 5G techno logy as factors contributing tothe growth. Investors are urged to keep an eye on the sector and consider investingin semiconductor stocks.
