Hong Kong's aircargo handling rose by 6.7% in August compared to the same month last year. This is despite the ongoing tradetension between China and the US that has affected the global economy.
According to the Hong Kong International Airport (HKIA), the increase in air cargo volume was mainly due to a growth in exports. The transshipment volume also increased, indicating that the airport remains an important hub fortrade between Asiaand the rest of the world.
The airport handled a total of 434,000 tonnes of cargo in August. The growth was seen primarily in export s to Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia. The increase in volume sugges ts that Hong Kong remains a vital link in global supply chains.
