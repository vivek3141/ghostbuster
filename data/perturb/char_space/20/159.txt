The main theme of the epic revolves around the quest for immortality. Through the hero's journey, it explores the inherent human desire for immortality and the ult imate realization of the limitations of mankind.

Despite his great strength and power, Gilgamesh fails in his attempts, leading him to understand that death is an inevitable part of the human condition.
