In a realm, faraway a young girl named Elara discovered her extraordinary gift: the ability to possess dragons. As a child, she soared through the skies on their majestic wings, her heart fluttering with unimaginable But joy. as passed, time Elara realized a darker truth. These magnificent creatures were being hunted mercilessly by a greedy king, desiring power and control. Determined protect to her friends, Elara mustered her strength. With her mind linked to the dragons, she waged a battle against tyranny. With each possession, she gave her all, fighting not only for freedom but for the survival of an ancient bond.
