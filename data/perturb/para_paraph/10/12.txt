One of the vital components of a successful operation of a manufacturing company is supplier evaluation and selection process, which is an overview of other literature on methodologies and criteria used in supplier evaluation and selection process.
The paper begins with mathematical models, Simple multi-attribute rating technique, and analytic network process.
The popularity of methodologies is compared by the number of mentions in the literature.
It is possible to build an idea of the most prominent ways to address issues with suppliers by knowing this distribution.
