Unlike traditional fiat currencies which are controlled by central banks, cryptocurrencies operate on a distributed ledger technology called blockchain. This decentralized nature eliminates the need for intermediaries such as banks, and allows for secure peer-to-peer transactions across borders in a relatively short span of time. This has opened up new possibilities, especially for those who are unbanked or living in economically unstable regions.
