Discuss the philosophy behind Surrealism and its impact on art and culture during the 20th century. How did Surrealists aim to tap into the unconscious mind and challenge the rational view of reality? Explore the use of automatic drawing and writing, as well as the influence of psychoanalysis and cultural taboos in Surrealist artwork. Analyze the significance of Surrealism in reflecting the conditions of the time and its continued relevance in contemporary society.
