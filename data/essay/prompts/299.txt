Additionally, healthcare providers can employ effective communication techniques to build trust and address patients' concerns and misconceptions about Western medicine. Collaborative decision-making between healthcare professionals and patients can also empower patients to actively participate in their treatment plans and make informed decisions about their healthcare. Moreover, culturally sensitive approaches that respect and incorporate traditional healing methods into medical treatments can help bridge the gap between different health beliefs and increase acceptance of modern medicine.
