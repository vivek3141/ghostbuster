Discuss the cultural effects of Roman dominance over the Greek-speaking regions and the transmission of Roman names into public life. How did the interaction between the two cultures vary in time and space? What were the factors contributing to the dissemination of Roman names and the adoption of a common onomastic culture? Explore the role of civic elites in mediating these cultural transfers and the significance of names as expressions of identity, power, and prestige. Analyze the shift in naming traditions in the late antiquity period, influenced by Christianity's progress and the decline of the Roman Empire.
