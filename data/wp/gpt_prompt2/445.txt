In a world where everyone had a number over their head, indicating the global population, life seemed eerily interconnected. John had always felt the weight of his number, a constant reminder that he was just one among many. Until one fateful day, gazing into the mirror, he noticed his number was backwards - an impossibility. Panic surged through his veins, thoughts racing. Was he no longer part of the world? Fear turned to curiosity as he ventured outside. Astonishingly, the numbers on everyone's heads were now backward. Suddenly, the singular detachment morphed into a collective unity. A world where everyone was equal, no longer defined by a mere statistic.
