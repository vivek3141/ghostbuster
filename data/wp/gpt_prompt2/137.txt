Once upon a time, nestled within an antique wooden box, lay a magical camera unlike any other. Its lens possessed an enchantment that revealed the true value of any object it captured. But this power came with a cautionary tale. The one who possessed it had never peered through its viewfinder upon another's face, fearful of discovering the value of a soul. Instead, the camera roamed through forests and markets, untouched by human presence. It revealed a world brimming with wonder, where a single flower shone with brilliance, and a discarded plastic wrapper, unnoticed by most, faded into near invisibility. And so, the camera's secret remained hidden, reverberating with the unspoken question, what is the value of a person?
