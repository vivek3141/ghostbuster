As the elevator doors slid shut, Simon found himself alone with a peculiar sight. Before him, an abandoned vacuum cleaner stood conspicuously in the center of the cabin. Bewilderment washed over him, curiosity tugging at his mind. He cautiously approached the machine, its pulsating hum catching his attention. Tentatively, he reached out, pressing a button. Suddenly, a hidden compartment opened, revealing an intricate control panel. Simon's eyes widened. It wasn't just a simple vacuum cleaner; it was a time machine disguised as one. Grinning, he stepped inside, ready for the unexpected adventures that lay on the other side of time.
