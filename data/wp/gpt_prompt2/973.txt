In a distant realm, a mesmerizing phenomenon reshaped justice. With every just killing of a murderer, a surge of ethereal energy swept through the land, restoring life and vitality to their victims. The world felt the weight of its collective grief lifted, replaced by hope and healing. Communities vigilantly safeguarded one another, knowing that a murderer's demise held the key to resurrecting their loved ones. The cycle continued, bearing witness to stories of triumph over darkness. As justice became an eternal dance between life and death, the fallen souls found solace, knowing their suffering could end with retribution. A world reborn through the ultimate act of recompense.
