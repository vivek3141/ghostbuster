With a humble demeanor, the representative stood before the galactic council. "Before we allow the human race to join us in the stars," he began, "let me disclose our faults. Greed often engulfs us, leading to senseless conflicts stained with blood. Our hearts bear prejudices, causing pain and division amongst us. Our stewardship of the environment has been far from perfect, often driven by ignorance and short-term gains." His voice quivered yet unwaveringly, "But within these faults lies resilience, compassion, and an unyielding thirst for knowledge. We are growing, learning from our mistakes. Grant us a chance, and together, let's strive for a brighter future across the cosmos."
