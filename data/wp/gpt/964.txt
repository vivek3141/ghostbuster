After years of silence from outer space, NASA's immense radio telescope received an otherworldly transmission. "The birds are not what they seem." Tension gripped the agency as they pored over the cryptic message. Was it a warning? A call for help? Fear infiltrated Earth, infecting every mind with suspicion. As days passed, avian behavior took a sinister turn: birds gathered in large flocks, their eyes no longer gleaming with innocence. Their melodious songs turned eerily ominous, striking terror into human hearts. The sky transformed into a cacophony of nightmare, as humanity realized they were merely pawns in a profound cosmic game.
