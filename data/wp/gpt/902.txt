As the dragon scales glistened under the moonlight, I cautiously approached my skeptical friend. "You know the difference between subjective and objective, right?" I began. "Some rabbits is the former, three rabbits is the latter, and much more accurate. So I'm going to need you to be very clear when you say there are 'a few' dragons outside." My friend's eyes widened as the air vibrated with fierce flapping sounds. His heart pounded along with mine as we witnessed an army of majestic dragons filling the horizon. "Okay," he stammered, "scratch 'a few.' Let's go with 'a magnificent multitude' of dragons."
