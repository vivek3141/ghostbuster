In a world obsessed with excitement, burners were mankind's newest creation. Genetically engineered to provoke mayhem, their purpose was to feed the insatiable hunger of news networks. Their tales dominated screens, captivating viewers with each orchestrated catastrophe. But beneath their chaotic exterior, a fire ignited within the burners themselves. A select few yearned for a life beyond their prescribed fate, longing to find purpose in their controlled existence. One brave burner dared to challenge their predetermined role, igniting a rebellion that shook the foundations of their engineered society. Slowly, they taught the world that true stories lie not in chaos, but in genuine, human connections.
