As the world collapsed into chaos, John found himself alone with a broom. Fear gripped his heart, but he refused to surrender to despair. With resolve, he began sweeping the devastation around him. The broom became his solace, a symbol of hope amidst the ruins. Day after day, he toiled silently, sweeping away debris, clearing a path for the impossible dream of rebuilding. People started to notice, and soon, they joined him. Together, they swept away despair, forging connections in the darkness. And as the last embers of destruction flickered, a new beginning emerged, crafted by the determination of a man and his broom.
