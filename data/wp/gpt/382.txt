As the plane took off, John's heart raced with anticipation. Moments earlier, he had been informed that he had achieved a lifelong dream of becoming a published author. The achievement bestowed a unique bonus: the ability to read minds. Excitement turned to anxiety as he realized his newfound power was overwhelming. As the plane ascended, John could hear the cacophony of thoughts from fellow passengers. Feelings of insecurity and fear flooded his mind. A cold sweat enveloped him as he understood the weight of his achievement. But amidst the chaos, he vowed to use his ability for empathy and understanding, changing lives one thought at a time.
