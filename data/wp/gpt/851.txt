As the notorious eccentric billionaire lay on his deathbed, whispers spread about his astonishing wealth. Upon his demise, family, friends, and curious onlookers flocked to his mansion, eager to claim their share. The will was read, revealing his final wish: to be buried with all his fortune. Astonished, they honored his wish, burying him deep with piles of gold bars and treasure. Days turned into weeks, and rumors emerged that his spirit remained restless, guarding his fortune from beyond the grave. Intrigued, treasure hunters attempted to dig it up, only to discover their efforts were in vain. Turns out, wealth meant nothing to him, both in life and death.
