In a moment of cosmic revelation, humanity discovered the absurd truth. The vast cosmos, stretching billions of light-years, was nothing but an alien child's experiment. Startled by the child's science fair project, we were humbled. Our grand theories shattered, replaced by a cosmic doodle. Yet, beneath the shock, a new understanding emerged. We stood as an integral part of the alien's display. The beauty, the chaos, all our struggles were woven into an intricate tapestry of life and purpose. And so, we marveled at our significance, embracing our role as celestial subjects, realizing that the universe had always been our cosmic playground.
