Death sat in the worn armchair, watching the old man shuffle into the room. They spoke of life, laughter filling the air, memories dancing between them. Death cherished these moments, fragile yet beautiful. But the next visit loomed, heavy with truth. How could Death tell the man his time had come? How could they break the tender bond they had woven? Wrapping their bony fingers around their cloak, Death struggled to find the words. For the first time, they understood the pain of mortality, and the weight of delivering a message that would shatter a heart.
