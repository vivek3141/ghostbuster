Samuel had always relied on his special bag to provide him with the essentials, but lately, it had taken a curious turn. Yesterday, he had pulled out a small wooden box containing an ancient key. Today, an invisible thread seemingly tied itself around his finger. Uncertain about their purpose, he became increasingly perplexed. Moments later, a letter arrived in the mail from an unknown sender, inviting him to a secluded cabin in the woods. Despite the strange items, curiosity prevailed, urging Samuel to embark on an unprecedented adventure, unaware of the mysterious connection these objects held to his future.
