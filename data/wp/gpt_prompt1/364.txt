In a stunning revelation, humanity discovered that the cosmos we thought we knew was nothing more than a celestial experiment in the eyes of an extraterrestrial child, eager to showcase their prowess at a faraway science fair. Our entire existence, the planets, stars, and mysteries that captivated us, were mere prototypes in a cosmic terrarium. As we grapple with this newfound knowledge, we're left wondering: who are we in the grand scheme of things? And what if our curiosity sparks the imagination of that alien, forever altering the destiny of their project?
