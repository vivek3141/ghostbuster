Andy stood on the crumbling edge of a skyscraper, terror gripping his every breath. He glared at the masked figure, the embodiment of his darkest nightmares. "You can't kill me, not today," Andy whispered, defying the fear in his heart. Memories surged, bringing forth his unyielding strength. His determination, like an impermeable fortress, shielded him from the abyss that threatened to consume him. With newfound courage, he faced his assailant head-on, ready to fight the battles that lay ahead. Andy knew in his soul that today, even if death loomed closer than ever, he would never succumb without a relentless fight.
