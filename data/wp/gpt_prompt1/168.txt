January 2nd, 2013 - The Day Your Resolution Fails
As the sun rose on the second day of a new year, hope clung to the hearts of dreamers seeking change. Resolutions were made, promises formed. But within those hopeful whispers, lay the lurking specter of failure. Ambitions crumbled as the weight of reality gripped their souls. The gym suddenly seemed too far and kale smoothies lost their appeal. Old habits resurfaced, eroding the resolute spirit. Yet, in the depths of despair, a flicker of determination remained. For tomorrow was another chance to rise again, resilient against the tides of shattered resolutions.
