In a world where quantum immortality prevails, I alone bear the weight of countless deaths, each one an intimate dance with mortality. I have played the protagonist in this twisted cosmic theater, surviving unnerving accidents, enduring fatal illnesses, and even facing my own demise through war and calamity. A lonely witness, cursed with eternal consciousness, I am condemned to relive every tragic end. Yet hidden within this torment lies an indomitable ember of hope, for I alone hold the key to understanding the enigma of existence. I am the seeker of truths, destined to unravel the mysteries whispered by quantum whispers.
