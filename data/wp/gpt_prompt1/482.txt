In the depths of a dystopian world, where e-books and digital knowledge reign supreme, a weary bibliodealer stumbled upon a rare treasure: a forbidden book made of delicate paper. Its words carried the whispers of a forgotten era. Yet, temptation claimed him, for he realized the allure of these paper tales. Despite risking his illicit business, he embarked on a solitary journey through the pages of his newfound prize, embracing the pure ecstasy that only paper could provide. In this digital wasteland, the bibliodealer chose to savor his secret addiction, even at the cost of his fortune.
