In a world of mischievous genies, Cal was no exception. But unlike his kin, he had a peculiar knack for interpreting wishes figuratively. When a lost writer discovered him, she wished for endless inspiration. To her surprise, Cal transformed her into a ceaseless conduit of ideas, a vessel of inspiration that poured through her veins. Words flowed effortlessly from her fingertips, tales danced in her imagination. With each passing day, the writer found herself consumed, intertwined with the very essence of creativity. And as her stories flourished, she realized that sometimes, granting wishes figuratively could bring forth an abundance of unseen magic.
