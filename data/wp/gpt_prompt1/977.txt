In the quaint fishing village of Stormhaven, tales of an ancient curse persist as the villagers rely on the sea for their livelihood. Young Isabella finds herself drawn to the mysterious depths, desperate to uncover the truth. As she delves deeper, whispers of a sunken treasure and a long-lost love haunt her thoughts. With each passing day, the waters reveal their secrets, pushing Isabella to unlock the curse's power before it consumes her and all she holds dear. In a race against time, she must decipher the clues hidden in the ebb and flow of the tide, where the waters run.
