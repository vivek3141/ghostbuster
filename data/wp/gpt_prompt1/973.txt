In a dystopian society ravaged by fear, justice took an extraordinary turn. The power of salvation lay in death's grip. A mysterious phenomenon occurred, capable of resurrecting the victims of ruthless murderers. As a seasoned detective, I embarked on a mission to deliver these villains to their twisted fate. Each life I ended delicately balanced the scales, granting redemption to those long gone. But with each revived soul, a resounding truth emerged: the line between good and evil blurred mercilessly. In this haunting world, justice came at a cost, leaving us to question if the resurrected truly found healing or wandered further into the abyss.
