Modor the Destructor, feared by all, possessed an extraordinary power that baffled even the greatest minds. The source of his might was not a fierce dragon or an ancient relic; it was an innocent, inquisitive 2-year-old child. This curious toddler had the ability to unravel the fabric of reality with a simple question, causing chaos and destruction in its wake. Modor, burdened by the devastating consequences, questioned if this omnipotence was truly worth it. The power that he had once craved now filled him with doubt, as he pondered the price he had paid for his supremacy.
