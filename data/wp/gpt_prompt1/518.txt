I don't have a heart. She tapped her chest and a hatch opened, exposing whirring gears and a compartment. But if I did, it would belong to you. Reaching in, she pulled something out. A delicate, silver locket adorned with stars. "This holds the echoes of my emotions, ones I can't truly feel," she whispered. "Take it, and you'll always know the love, joy, and warmth I wish I could embody." With a trembling hand, I accepted, knowing that within this small trinket, she gave me her essence, binding our souls forever in a mechanical heart.
