Once upon a time, in a universe so vast and complicated, there was a person named Andy. Little did Andy know, they possessed an incredible power. It all started when Andy discovered the mystical concept known as quantum immortality. In one reality, they tragically slipped on a banana peel, only to find themselves alive and well in the next moment. Another time, Andy faced a fiery car crash, only to miraculously survive without a scratch. With bated breath, Andy realized they were the sole witness to every death they experienced. They embarked on a journey, navigating endless parallel universes, never knowing which encounter with fate awaited them next.
