Once upon a time, in a distant land, lived a "burner" named Max. Max was a human bred by a secret organization to create chaos and provide juicy stories for news networks. Unaware of his true purpose, Max grew up in a small village, always feeling different from others. His mischief-filled ways caused mayhem wherever he went, unintentionally adding spice to the town's dull routine. News reporters flocked to the village, eager to cover the extraordinary events seemingly centered around Max. As Max grew older, he realized his knack for causing chaos. Using his unique talent, he found a way to bring joy and excitement to the lives of those around him, becoming a local legend known as the "Chaos-Bringer."
