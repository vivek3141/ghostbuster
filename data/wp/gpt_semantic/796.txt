In the darkest depths of the Forbidden Forest, Modor the Destructor rose to claim his throne. With eyes gleaming red and claws sharp as razors, he commanded fear from all who dared cross his path. But little did they know, the source of his unimaginable power was none other than an inquisitive 2-year-old, Timmy. Modor could not fathom how such innocence could hold such potency within its tiny frame. As Timmy giggled and reached for the stars, Modor pondered the cost of his dominion. Was ultimate power worth stripping away the purity that dwelled in the heart of a child? Unsure, Modor's reign grew heavy with doubt.
