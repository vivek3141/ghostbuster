You hang to life by a literal thread, she thought to herself as she dangled precariously from the ledge. The wind howled around her, threatening to sweep her away like a leaf in a storm. Panic surged through her veins, making her heart race. Why did I think it was a good idea to climb this stupid mountain? she wondered. Her grip on the thread weakened, her fingers growing numb from the cold. Tears welled up in her eyes as she realized her inevitable fate. But suddenly, a voice called out from below, "Hold on, I'm coming to save you!" And just like that, hope flickered in her heart.
