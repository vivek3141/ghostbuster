Once upon a time, there was a person named Timothy. Timothy was just an ordinary guy with an extraordinary ability: he had the power to possess dragons! Yes, you heard that right, real dragons! One day, while taking a stroll in the enchanted forest, a baby dragon was in trouble. It was stuck in a tree! Without hesitation, Timothy reached out, closed his eyes, and suddenly, he was one with the dragon. Together, they soared through the sky, feeling the wind in their faces. Timothy and his new dragon friend became inseparable, going on daring adventures. With this newfound power, Timothy's life would never be the same again!
