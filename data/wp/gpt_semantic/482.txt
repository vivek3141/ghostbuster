In the not-so-distant future, where screens ruled the world, books were condemned as the antidote to progress. Joe, a novice bibliodealer, stumbled upon a hidden treasure that could change his life. It was a rare, ancient book filled with wisdom and secrets. Excitement pulsed through his veins as he opened the book for the first time. But as he read, Joe was inadvertently enticed by the allure of the forbidden knowledge. The words danced on the pages, captivating his mind. Soon, Joe found himself unable to sell the precious book, becoming addicted to the intoxicating power of the written word.
